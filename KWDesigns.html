About Me

Hi there! my name is Kristoffer Williams, I am a passionate and dedicated graphic designer with a focus on delivering creative solutions that make an impact. My journey as a designer has been shaped by a love for storytelling and visual communication, which drives me to craft designs that resonate with audiences and fulfill client objectives.

My Projects

Future Funds

As a UX Designer for a school project, I designed the homepage of FutureFunds, a fictional financial planning service tailored to young professionals. My role involved creating a clean, modern interface with an intuitive navigation structure and clear visual hierarchy to enhance user engagement. I emphasized accessibility and clarity in the content layout, using calming colors and relatable illustrations to make financial services feel approachable. This project allowed me to apply user-centered design principles to communicate trust and credibility while improving the overall usability and aesthetic of a financial service platform.

Fresh Fare Farms

As a UX Designer for a school project with Fresh Fare Farms as the focus, I created a user-centered promotional flyer to simulate a real-world campaign promoting sustainable meal kits. The goal was to design an engaging and accessible layout that clearly communicated the brand’s mission while encouraging user participation. I applied core UX principles such as visual hierarchy, intuitive flow, and emotional appeal through the use of compelling imagery, readable typography, and strategically placed calls to action. The project emphasized community impact by highlighting local sourcing, environmental benefits, and a Hunger Action Month initiative. This school project allowed me to practice designing with purpose and empathy, ultimately strengthening my skills in crafting user-friendly marketing materials.

Donnt T

As the designer and typographic illustrator for this piece, I created a text-based portrait using carefully arranged lyrics to form the contours and details of the subject's face and upper body. My role involved blending visual storytelling with expressive typography to turn words into imagery. The impact of this work lies in its ability to merge language and likeness into a cohesive artistic statement—demonstrating how text can function as both communication and composition. It highlights my skills in creative layout, type manipulation, and conceptual design.

Horizon Railways

As the UX/UI Designer for this Horizon Railways ticket interface, I focused on creating a clean, accessible layout that clearly communicates essential travel details. I designed the visual hierarchy to prioritize user needs—highlighting departure info, seat and platform details, and ticket features like WiFi. The use of icons, bold typography, and minimal color accents supports quick scanning and mobile usability. This design improves the user experience by reducing confusion and ensuring key information is easy to find at a glance, contributing to smoother travel and better customer satisfaction.

Scoop Shoppe

As the Visual and UX Designer for Scoop Shoppe’s promotional campaign, I created a vibrant, eye-catching layout that highlights the brand’s artisanal and wholesome values. I designed the visual structure, selected playful imagery, and crafted clear messaging to communicate product quality, special offers, and brand personality. The result was a cohesive, user-friendly promotional piece that effectively engaged audiences and enhanced brand recognition, while supporting customer conversion through a strong call-to-action.
